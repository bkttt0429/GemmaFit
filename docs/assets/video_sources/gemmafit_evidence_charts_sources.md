## Claims and Evidence

1. Taiwan became a super-aged society by the end of 2025.
   - Claim used: 65+ population was 4,673,155, or 20.06% of total population.
   - Source: Ministry of the Interior household registration statistics, reported by CNA.
   - URL: https://www.cna.com.tw/news/ahel/202601090098.aspx

2. Sarcopenia prevalence varies substantially by definition.
   - Claim used: international community-dwelling older-adult estimates range from 9.9% to 40.4%; EWGSOP/AWGS pooled estimate 12.9%.
   - Source: Mayhew et al., Age and Ageing systematic review and meta-analysis.
   - URL: https://academic.oup.com/ageing/article/48/1/48/5058979

3. Taiwan older-adult low calf circumference screening proxy.
   - Claim used: Taiwan 65+ low calf circumference proxy was 34.1% in the cited nutrition survey report.
   - Important limitation: this is a screening proxy, not a clinical sarcopenia diagnosis.
   - Source: Taiwan nutrition and older-adult nutrition monitoring report.
   - URL: https://crc.sfaa.gov.tw/Uploadfile/StatiscsKnowledge/34_20220629145208_2497642.pdf

4. Falls among older adults in Taiwan.
   - Claim used: falls ranked second among accidental injury death causes for people aged 65+; mortality rate 25.2 per 100,000.
   - Source: Health Promotion Administration, Ministry of Health and Welfare.
   - URL: https://www.hpa.gov.tw/Pages/Detail.aspx?nodeid=807&pid=20033

5. Sarcopenia and fall risk.
   - Claim used: sarcopenia was associated with higher fall risk; pooled OR 1.52 overall and OR 1.69 among community-dwelling older adults.
   - Source: Zhang et al., Clinical Nutrition meta-analysis, PubMed PMID 30665817.
   - URL: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/30665817/

6. Exercise recommendations for older adults.
   - Claim used: older adults should do muscle-strengthening activity on 2 or more days per week and varied multicomponent balance/strength activity on 3 or more days per week.
   - Source: WHO Guidelines on Physical Activity and Sedentary Behaviour.
   - URL: https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/books/NBK566046/

7. Community care center count.
   - Claim used: Taiwan had 4,828 community care centers as of September 2023.
   - Source: Social and Family Affairs Administration, Ministry of Health and Welfare.
   - URL: https://www.mohw.gov.tw/dl-86492-a1e4ee47-db85-450a-b22d-5da18bf54dd5.html
