## 1. Overview

Use the **Gemma 4** model to create a solution that tackles real-world challenges — whether it's an app that helps millions, or a specialized model that scales innovation.

### Key Themes

| Theme | Description |
|-------|-------------|
| **Health & Sciences** | Bridge the gap between people and data. Build tools that accelerate discovery or democratize knowledge. |
| **Global Resilience** | Build future-proof systems — from offline edge-based disaster response to long-term climate mitigation. |
| **Future of Education** | Reimagine learning with multi-tool agents that adapt to individuals and empower educators. |
| **Digital Equity & Inclusion** | Break barriers through language diversity, intuitive interfaces, and tools that close the AI skills gap. |
| **Safety & Trust** | Pioneer frameworks for transparency and reliability, ensuring AI remains pragmatic and explainable. |

## 2. Submission Requirements

A valid submission **must** contain all of the following:

### 2a. Kaggle Writeup (≤ 1500 words)
Your project report / technical proof. Must clearly articulate:
- Application architecture
- How you use Gemma 4
- Challenges overcome
- Why your technical choices are correct

> Exceeding 1500 words may result in penalties.

### 2b. Public Video (≤ 3 minutes, YouTube)
The **most important** part of your submission. Tell a compelling story:
- What problem are you solving?
- How does your Gemma 4 app solve it powerfully?
- Show real-world impact.

> Must be publicly accessible without login.

### 2c. Public Code Repository
"Source of truth" — GitHub, Kaggle Notebook, etc. Must:
- Be well-documented
- Clearly demonstrate Gemma 4 implementation
- Be publicly accessible without login or paywall

### 2d. Live Demo
A URL or uploaded file providing hands-on experience:
- Publicly accessible (no login / no paywall)
- APK, web app, or downloadable artifact

### 2e. Media Gallery
Upload images and/or videos related to your submission. A **cover image** is required.

## 4. Evaluation Criteria

| Criteria (Score) | Description |
|-------------------|-------------|
| **Impact & Vision** (40 pts) | How clearly and powerfully does your project solve an important real-world problem? Is the vision inspiring? Does it have genuine potential for positive change? |
| **Video Pitch & Storytelling** (30 pts) | Is the video compelling, engaging, and well-produced? Does it tell a captivating story that grabs the audience? |
| **Technical Depth & Execution** (30 pts) | How innovatively are Gemma 4's unique capabilities used (verified via codebase & docs)? Is the technology real, functional, and well-designed — not just faked for the demo? |

> Your project is **primarily judged on the video demo**. All projects must have working technical backing verified through code and documentation.
